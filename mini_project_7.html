Movies that inspire · Books that linger · Food that comforts

🎬 Favorite Movies
🎞️ Film
Inception (2010)
A mind-bending heist thriller by Christopher Nolan. Dream layers, spinning tops, and an emotional core that stays with you long after the credits.
🍃 Studio Ghibli
Spirited Away
Hayao Miyazaki’s masterpiece about courage, identity, and the spirit world. Stunning animation, unforgettable characters — a magical journey.
⭐ Sci‑Fi Classic
Interstellar
A breathtaking voyage through wormholes and time dilation. Hans Zimmer’s score + emotional father-daughter story make it an epic about love and survival.
📚 Favorite Books
📖 Fantasy
The Name of the Wind
Patrick Rothfuss weaves lyrical prose and deep worldbuilding. Follow Kvothe through music, magic, and mystery — a slow-burn masterpiece.
🏆 Pulitzer Winner
The Overstory
Richard Powers connects nine strangers through their love for trees. An eco-epic that changes how you see forests and human interconnectedness.
🐉 Sci‑Fi
Dune
Frank Herbert’s desert epic of politics, religion, and ecology. Arrakis, spice, and the Fremen — a dense, brilliant saga that inspired generations.
🍽️ Favorite Foods
🍕 Italian Classic
Margherita Pizza
Simple perfection: San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, basil, and a thin crispy crust. Every bite feels like a trip to Naples.
🍣 Japanese Delicacy
Sushi (Nigiri & Rolls)
Fresh fish, perfectly seasoned rice, and a touch of wasabi. From buttery salmon to crunchy tempura rolls — light and indulgent.
🍫 Dessert Heaven
Chocolate Lava Cake
Warm, gooey dark chocolate center that flows like molten gold. Served with vanilla ice cream — pure bliss.
